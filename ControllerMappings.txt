            *HTC VIVE controller*
Right Contoller:
Touch Pad 'North' Touch = Pointing ray activation.
Touch Pad 'North' Click = Object Dragging activation.
Touch Pad 'South' Click = Toggle between Flying/Walking.

Left Contoller:
Touch Pad 'North' Touch = Teleportation ray activation.
Touch Pad 'North' Click = Teleportation.
Touch Pad 'East' Click = Right Snap Turn.
Touch Pad 'West' Click = Left Snap Turn.
